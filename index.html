RAH 'SHUN BALL

Professional MMA Fighter & Combatives Engineer

  • About

    Rah 'Shun Ball is a lifelong martial artist with a combined 15 years of training and teaching. He has studied a variety of different styles including Kyokushin karate and Capoeira. For the past few years, he has focused on perfecting his grappling and striking techniques with Brazilian jiu jitsu and Shotokan karate.

    Rah 'Shun made his professional debut at XFC 22's "Crossing the Line" event and has a professional record of 1-0.
